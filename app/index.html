Who is Patriot Pup?

Patriot Pup is the first in a series of children's books

patriotism, loyality, and friendship.

About the Author

Meet Elisa M. Camara!

She was born and raised in West Virginia, went to Queens University in
Charlotte, North Carolina, and is currently located in Orlando, Florida. She is
the mother of three sons and this sister to a fallen soldier

Elisa was first inspired by her brother, Sergeant Mecot Camara. She
published her first book, American Brother in 2013 See Here

Elisa put her creative writing skills, her love of children and her love of our
country together to set fire to the creation of Patriot Pup.

Patriot Pup has teamed up with an organization called Just Our Soldier's Helpers or
